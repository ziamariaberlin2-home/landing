More than just a pizzeria

Where culinary art meets contemporary culture

A piece of Berlin's heart

Zia Maria is where a passion for pizza meets art and a warm sense of community. Born as a family project, our restaurant combines the tradition of Italian pizza with a friendly, creative atmosphere. We believe that every pizza is a work of art—made with fresh ingredients and a light, flavorful dough—and that food brings people together. We showcase local artists with rotating exhibitions, transforming our space into a vibrant gallery where guests can savor contemporary flavors and find inspiration. At Zia Maria, you come to eat well, feel at home, and discover something beautiful.

Made with love since 2020

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Zia Maria

Do you offer delivery?

Currently, we only offer pickup. You can place your order online and pick it up at our restaurant.

How long will it take to prepare my order?

Our pizzas are freshly prepared and usually take 15-20 minutes. We will notify you when your order is ready for pickup.

Do you have vegan and vegetarian options?

Absolutely! We offer a wide variety of vegan and vegetarian pizzas made with fresh, high-quality ingredients. Check out our menu for the full selection.

Can I customize my pizza?

Yes! You can add or remove ingredients when you order. We also offer gluten-free options and can meet most dietary requirements.

What are your opening hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 12:00 to 22:00 and on Sundays from 12:00 to 21:00.

How can I pay for my order?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and cash payments. Online orders can be paid securely through our checkout system.
