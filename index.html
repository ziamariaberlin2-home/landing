Our Story

Zia Maria is your neighborhood Italian in the heart of Friedrichshain. We specialize in Roman-style pizza, crafted with a long-fermentation dough that's light, crispy, and incredibly flavorful. Our ethos is simple: seasonal ingredients, community spirit, and authentic taste.
